Vision: Provide an engaging platform were software engineers can reinforce their technical and behavioral interviewing skills.

Team Policies:
- Keep it cool
- Majority rules  
- Keep the ball rolling

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Features:

Top down two dimensional board

Optional Local Multiplayer

Single Player

Players can select a game piece 

Players will be able to create an account and store their data

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Single Player Gameplay Loop)
-Start of Turn-
Roll Dice to determine where what tile you land on
-Action Phase-
Depending on the tile you landed on you may:
    - Purchase the Property
    - Draw From Chance Deck
    - Draw From Community Chest Deck
    - Nothing Happens
    - Get Laid Off 
-Development Phase-
Only if you own a monopoly:
    - Select the monopoly you want to attempt to develop
    - Answer a question, the difficulty of which is equivalent to the difficulty of the property
    If your answer is correct and you do not have 4 houses:
        - you gain a house on that property
    If your answer is correct and you do have 4 houses:
        - you gain a hotel on that property
    If your answer is incorrect:
        - nothing happens
-Next Turn Begins-

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Single Player :
Cease Fire System
- Before game, select Small, Medium or Large game
- Depending on that selection, a game is initialized with a predetermined amount of turns
- At the end of those turns: the player is congratulated and given their score
- Score is determined by the amount of properties you own and the difficulty level of your properties

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Rules For:

Determining Ownership)
-do you get the question correct-
Yes) you get to own the property
No) you do not get to own the property 


Determining Score)
The amount of score gained/lost is determined by the difficulty of the question answered 
- Land on property unowned -
  - Correct) add score 
  - Incorrect) Score remains the same
- Land on property owned -
  - Correct) Score remains the same
  - Incorrect) Score decrements

Landing on a railroad)
Just like a monopoly property, it is either owned or unowned.
Land on Owned: Spin a wheel, determines question; LeetCode, Behavioral or System Design. If you answer the question wrong your score decrements depending on if the owner owns:
    - 1 RR) -25 pts
    - 2 RR) -50pts
    - 1 RR) -100pts
    - 2 RR) -200pts 
Land on unowned: Spin a wheel, determines question; LeetCode, Behavioral or System Design. Answer the question to determine ownership.

Landing on an easy monopoly property)
If the property is:
owned) The player will be prompted to answer an easy question from the predetermined question type.
    If the answer is correct:
        - The player will have 0 score taken from them and the owner will gain 0 score
    If the answer is incorrect check if the owner has:
        - 0 houses: player -10pts, owner +10pts
        - 1 houses: player -50pts, owner +50pts
        - 2 houses: player -150pts, owner +150pts
        - 3 houses: player -450pts, owner +450pts
        - 4 houses: player -750pts, owner +750pts
        - 1 hotel: player -1150pts, owner +1150pts
unowned) The player will be prompted to answer an easy question from the predetermined question type. 
    If the answer is correct:
        - The player will be granted ownership of the property
    If the answer is incorrect: 
        - The player will not be granted ownership of the property
